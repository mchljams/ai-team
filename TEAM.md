is designed to be reusable — project-specific context lives in repo-level docs, not in
team personas.

## Decisions Log

### 2026-04-08 — Program Director as first role
**Decision:** The first role defined is Program Director.
**Options considered:** Technical Director, Chief of Staff, Program Director.
**Rationale:** Program Director best reflects the facilitative, initiative-wide nature
of the role. It implies overseeing the whole AI team program rather than being tied
to a single project or being prescriptive in approach.
**Decided by:** mchljams

### 2026-04-08 — Naming convention established
**Decision:** All personas are given Irish/Gaeilge names.
**Rationale:** Personal to the human lead. Names are proposed by the persona in its
inaugural session. The human lead makes the final choice.
**Decided by:** mchljams

### 2026-04-08 — Team changelog adopted
**Decision:** A `CHANGELOG.md` tracks role evolution and team milestones.
**Rationale:** Mirrors existing discipline in `hptuners_Phoenix`. Keeps `PD.md` clean
and `TEAM.md` strategic while giving the initiative a record of progression.
**Decided by:** mchljams
